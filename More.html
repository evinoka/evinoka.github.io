Events on Campus

LCC is not merely interested at presenting academic competence but also introducing students to different leadership positions and a variety of different events where anyone can participate. Some of these events include Cultural days, spirtual events such as retereat, alpha course faith and discussion etc, Trivia night, Talent night, Ted talks, etc. Many students are engaged in creating and managing different events. Some of those students help volunterarly and others commit to a leadership position. Some of the leadership positions that LCC present imclude Spiritual Life, FYS, Resident Assisstant, Admission councelor, Interculture team, study abroad intern, and exchange program.
