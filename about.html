LCC International University

Currently I am a third year student and I study Contemporary Communication in LCC International University. My university is located in Klaipeda, Lithuania. In addtion of being a full-time student, I am also an FYS leader,where my primarily task is to engage with a specific group of freshmen. In terms of sport activities, I am part of LCC football team. Recently I have created my onw initiative "VOX Pop LCC", where together with my friends I interview LCC students about different topics. I love my show simply because it is fun interacting with people here and getting to know them through a simple camera.

Albania

I was born and raised in Albania. I left my country at the age of 17 to study in Lithuania. Albania is located in the southeastern part of Europe by the Adriatic and Ionian see. Geographically, Albania is a very rich country as it is surrounded by beautiful mountains, fields, hills, seas, lakes, and rivers. Moreover, Albania is a tourist and hospitable country. During the summer season, many foreigners choose to spend their vacation in Albania, especially in the coastal cities. During the summer many tourists visit the historical museums, castles, go for hiking etc .

Lithuania

Currently, I am living and studying in Lithuania,Klaipeda. I moved here at the age of 17 and it is already been my third year living here. Lithuania is located in the eastern part of Europe and it is known for the beautiful Baltic sea. I am in love with the architecture and the medieval Old Town in Vilnius. In my free time I usually go for walking in the parks, visit public library, or go to the beach. I also love the coffe shops and someitme I spend quite a big amount of time studying there. I consider myself lucky to live here and share so many unique experiences.
